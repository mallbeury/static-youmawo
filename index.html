How It Works

Guaranteed to find your perfect fitting glasses.

Made to order, bespoke eyewear designed to flawlessly fit your face. We use your unique facial measurements to produce a customized fit you won’t find in frames bought off the rack.

Our Glasses

  • Design

    All our designs are technically and dynamically optimized based on analysis of hundreds of faces.

    Learn more

  • Scan

    The bases for each custom tailored eyewear frame is the recording of your individual face topography.

    Learn more

  • Custom Tailoring

    Our self-developed algorithms enable you to have the glasses dynamically adapted your individual face.

    Learn more

  • Manufacturing

    YOU MAWO manufactures the highest quality, with the most advanced production technology in the world.

    Learn more

  • Go To Market

    Our frames are exclusively sold via select opticians.

    Learn more

  • Technology

    Our software combines cutting edge 3D scanning, 3D printing, and machine learning to create custom tailored products across different markets.

    Learn more
