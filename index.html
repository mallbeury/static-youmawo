How It Works

Guaranteed to find your perfect fit.

Made to order, bespoke eyewear designed to flawlessly fit your face. We use your unique facial measurements to produce a customized fit you won’t find in frames bought off the rack.

Shop Glasses

  • Design

    All our designs are technically and dynamically optimized based on analysis of hundreds of faces.

    Learn more

  • Scan

    The bases for each custom tailored eyewear frame is the recording of your individual face topography.

    Learn more

  • Custom Tailoring

    Our self-developed algorithms enable you to have the frame dynamically adapted your individual face.

    Learn more

  • Manufacture

    YOU MAWO manufactures the highest quality, most advanced production technology in the world.

    Learn more

  • Go to Market

    Our frames are exclusively sold via select opticians, normally delivered in under 15 working days.

    Learn more

  • Propietary Technology

    Our proprietary software that enables us to manage complexity from order to payment for bespoke products at scale.

    Learn more
Technology

Our patented 3D scan production process.

Our SLS printer delivers the exact features you look for in eyewear – a comfortable, nuanced design suited to contours of the face and an industry-leading build quality.
